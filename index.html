About Game

‘The Dragons’ game is the best way to bring up your own Dragon. All you have to do is just buy the Dragon egg and wait till it hatches. Then you lead the newborn Dragon through all life stages. It may take part in battles, use mutagen and change its DNA characteristics, mate and create new dragons. You may also sell your Dragon or give it to somebody.

Start playing and find yourself in a breathtaking and fabulous
Dragon’s World!

Features

DNA is the set of Dragon’s natural characteristics which is generated
at the moment of its creation. DNA is responsible for your Dragon
appearance and battle characteristics. You may change the DNA only
by using mutagen.

The egg is the first stage of Dragon’s evolution. You may see the
glimpse of the appearance of your Dragon on this stage.

The Dragon is the main element of the Game, which has the unique
appearance and fighting qualities. It may be born as the descendant
of its parents or you may also create it in the first generation.

Battle

Due to DNA including a lot of fighting characteristics, lovely creatures turn into fearless warriors.
Winning Dragon gets 10 Mutagen Tokens, losing warrior receives 1 token. DANGER! The Dragon
may die after losing the fatal battle.

Breed

Such options as breeding and dynasties will be available soon. The Dragons can reproduce
themselves and the youngs inherit parents’ DNA. You may also conduct an auction
and cross your Dragon with the other one. The Dragon needs recreation time after the
copulation.

Deals

You may sell your unique Dragon at the auction. Each Dragon has its
own unique characteristics and appearance.

GRAVEYARD

It’s the place where Dragons appear after the last battle or after
using a fatal dose of mutagen. You can not cure your Dragon
anymore.
